Revolutionizing Heart Health with Real-Time Monitoring and AI Insights

Experience the future of healthcare with our cutting-edge wearable device and web platform. By seamlessly collecting real-time ECG data through advanced sensors, our system leverages machine learning to detect potential heart conditions instantly. Stay connected and informed with live notifications that include location updates, ensuring peace of mind for patients, families, and doctors. Explore real-time ECG waves, past health insights, and live tracking through our intuitive web app. Redefine cardiac care with technology designed to save lives and empower health decisions.

How it works Learn more

CARDIOLOGY

A wearable ecg device, which sends the patients ecg data at real time to the cloud, the data is processed in the wifi powered microcontroller.

ELECTROCARDIOGRAM

Each patient will have his own storage of the ECG data Machine learning is used in the cloud to provide real time disease detection.

ACTIVE LOCATION

Real time location of the patient can be viewed by the caretaker on the webapp.

ALERTS

Notifications are shared to the caretaker and doctor, If a patient is diagonsed with a disease Notified along with the patients location.
